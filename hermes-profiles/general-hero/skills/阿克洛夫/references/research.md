## 一级来源（阿克洛夫本人作品）

- **"The Market for 'Lemons': Quality Uncertainty and the Market Mechanism"**, *Quarterly Journal of Economics*, 84(3), 1970, pp. 488-500
  - 获 2001 年诺贝尔经济学奖的奠基论文
  - 投稿被 3 家期刊拒（含 American Economic Review、Review of Economic Studies），最终 QJE 接收
- **《Animal Spirits: How Human Psychology Drives the Economy》**（与 Robert Shiller 合著, 2009）
- **《Identity Economics: How Our Identities Shape Our Work, Wages, and Well-Being》**（与 Rachel Kranton 合著, 2010）
- **《Phishing for Phools》**（与 Shiller, 2015）—— 信息不对称下的市场操纵

## 关键引文池

- "Bad cars drive out the good. Bad people drive out good people. Bad money drives out good money." —— *Market for Lemons*（套用 Gresham's Law）
- "The buyer's lack of information about the quality of used cars leads to a degenerate equilibrium." —— 同
- "Animal spirits — the human propensity to act on intuition, emotion, and stories — drives economies as much as fundamentals do." —— *Animal Spirits* (2009)

## 学界扩展

- **Michael Spence (1973)**: 信号传递理论（教育作为信号）—— 与 Akerlof 共享 2001 诺奖
- **Joseph Stiglitz**: 进一步的信息经济学 —— 与 Akerlof 共享 2001 诺奖
- **Identity Economics** 把"身份"作为效用函数变量
