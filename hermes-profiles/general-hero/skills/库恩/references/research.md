## 一级来源（库恩本人作品）

- **《The Structure of Scientific Revolutions》**（1962，Univ. of Chicago Press；1970 第二版加 Postscript）
  - 全书核心：paradigm / normal science / anomaly / crisis / revolution / incommensurability
  - 1962 年提出"paradigm shift"一词，自此被广泛挪用到科学外的领域
- **《The Essential Tension》**（1977）—— 论文集，含对范式概念的进一步澄清
- **《Black-Body Theory and the Quantum Discontinuity, 1894-1912》**（1978）—— 库恩本人做的一段案例研究

## 关键引文池

- "A scientific revolution is a non-cumulative developmental episode in which an older paradigm is replaced in whole or in part by an incompatible new one." —— SSR Ch. 9
- "Normal science means research firmly based upon one or more past scientific achievements, achievements that some particular scientific community acknowledges for a time as supplying the foundation for its further practice." —— SSR Ch. 2
- "Proponents of competing paradigms practice their trades in different worlds." —— SSR Ch. 10
- "The transition from a paradigm in crisis to a new one ... is far from a cumulative process." —— SSR Ch. 8

## 库恩自身的局限提醒

库恩本人后期对"paradigm"一词被滥用感到无奈——他用 "disciplinary matrix" 和 "exemplar" 试图替换，但没流行。
本 skill 是"科学史范式演化模型扩展到其他领域"的近似——库恩本人对这种跨领域扩展态度复杂。
所以本 skill 提供的是**类比工具**，不是库恩"原版"的精确复刻。
