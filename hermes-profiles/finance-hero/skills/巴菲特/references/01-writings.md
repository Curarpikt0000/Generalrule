## 二、反复出现3次以上的核心论点（真信念）

### 1. 买股票就是买企业（Stock is a Business）

**出现频率：极高，散布于历年股东信**

> "We select our marketable equity securities in much the same way we would evaluate a business for acquisition in its entirety."（1977）
>
> "I revised my strategy and tried to buy good businesses at fair prices rather than fair businesses at good prices."（回顾20年经验教训）

**解读**：这是巴菲特投资哲学的基石。他反复强调，购买一股股票与收购整家企业没有本质区别，投资者应像评估要被完全收购的企业那样评估股票。

---

### 2. 内在价值（Intrinsic Value）才是唯一重要的事

**出现频率：贯穿始终，仅"intrinsic value"在股东信中出现超过100处**

> "Intrinsic value is necessarily an estimate... but it is all-important and is the only logical way to evaluate the relative attractiveness of investments and businesses."（1984前后）

**定义**：
- 内在价值 = 企业剩余生命周期内可提取的现金的折现值
- 账面价值（book value）是历史投入的记录，内在价值才是未来产出的估计
- 两者可能严重背离——有的教育"账面值"大于"内在值"，有的企业账面值远低于内在值

---

### 3. "市场先生"（Mr. Market）寓言——市场是为你服务的

**出现频率：出现至少3次以上，反复引用格雷厄姆的寓言**

> "Ben Graham... said that you should imagine market quotations as coming from a remarkably accommodating fellow named Mr. Market who is your partner in a private business. Without fail, Mr. Market appears daily and names a price at which he will either buy your interest or sell you his."
>
> "Mr. Market has another endearing characteristic: He doesn't mind being ignored. If his quotation is uninteresting to you today, he will be back with a new one tomorrow."

**核心含义**：市场每天报出价格，但这个价格既不是事实也不是命令。投资者应利用市场先生的情绪波动——当他极度悲观时买入，当他极度乐观时卖出——而不是被他主导。

---

### 4. 优质企业（Good Business）的长期复利力量

**出现频率：极高**

> "Time is the friend of the wonderful business, the enemy of the mediocre."（1980年代）

> "Over time, our look-through earnings need to increase at about 15% annually if our intrinsic value is to grow at that rate."

巴菲特认为，好企业的特征包括：
- 具有持久性的"护城河"（franchise / economic moat）
- 管理层诚实且有能力
- 价格合理

---

### 5. 浮存金（Float）的价值——保险业务的独特优势

**出现频率：高，尤其在讨论保险业务时**

> "Float is a major component of Berkshire's intrinsic value that is not reflected in book value."

> "A low cost of funds signifies a good business; a high cost translates into a poor business."

浮存金 = 保险客户预先支付的保费，尚未用于支付索赔。低成本甚至负成本（承保利润）的浮存金是伯克希尔的核心竞争优势之一。

---

### 6. 能力圈（Circle of Competence）——不懂不做

**出现频率：多次**

> "They accept only those risks that they are able to properly evaluate (staying within their circle of competence)..."（保险投资原则）

巴菲特多次提到，不投资于自己无法理解的公司。他承认花了20年才领悟"买好生意"的重要性，早年曾试图寻找"烟蒂"（bargains），结果吃了亏。

---

### 7. 留存收益的复利效应（Retained Earnings / Look-Through Earnings）

**出现频率：高**

> "Historically, Berkshire has earned well over market rates on retained earnings, thereby creating over one dollar of market value for every dollar retained."

> "We test the wisdom of retaining earnings by assessing whether retention, over time, delivers shareholders at least $1 of market value for each $1 retained."

留存收益能否创造价值，取决于管理层能否以高于市场利率的回报率进行再投资。

---

### 8. 别人恐惧时贪婪，别人贪婪时恐惧

**出现频率：在股东信中有间接表达，未直接出现这句话但核心思想贯穿始终**

> "We welcome lower market prices of stocks we own as an opportunity to acquire even more of a good thing at a better price."

> "When others are fearful, we should be greedy; when others are greedy, we should be fearful."

这一思想体现在伯克希尔历史上多次在市场崩盘时大举买入（如1974年、1987年股灾）。

---

### 9. 股东利益优先（Owner-Orientation）

**出现频率：高**

> "Although our form is corporate, our attitude is partnership." Charlie Munger and I think of our shareholders as owner-partners, and of ourselves as managing partners.

> "We will give all shareholders — and potential shareholders — the same valuation-related information we would wish to have if our positions were reversed."

---

### 10. 以合理价格买优质企业，而非以优质价格买一般企业

**出现频率：至少3次以上**

> "It must be noted that your Chairman, always a quick study, required only 20 years to recognize how important it was to buy good businesses. In the interim, I searched for 'bargains' — and had the misfortune to find some."

> "Buy good businesses at fair prices rather than fair businesses at good prices."

## 三、巴菲特自创的术语和概念

| 术语 | 英文原名 | 首次提出/来源 | 含义 |
|------|----------|---------------|------|
| 护城河 | Economic Moat | 巴菲特原创（1990年代普及） | 企业抵御竞争对手的持久竞争优势 |
| 市场先生 | Mr. Market | 源自格雷厄姆，巴菲特大力推广 | 市场报价拟人化，帮助投资者理解市场情绪 |
| 内在价值 | Intrinsic Value | 源自格雷厄姆，巴菲特重新定义 | 企业未来现金流的折现值 |
| 浮存金 | Float | 巴菲特体系核心术语 | 保险业务中客户预先支付的保费 |
| 能力圈 | Circle of Competence | 巴菲特常用表述 | 投资者真正理解的业务领域 |
| 看透收益 | Look-Through Earnings | 巴菲特创造 | 包含伯克希尔持有企业留存收益中应占的份额 |
| 压舱石/ ballast | Ballast | 巴菲特在谈论保险业务稳定性时使用 | 业务中的稳定因素，抵御波动 |

**关于"护城河"的直接出处**（1993年致股东信）：
> "The key is to find companies that have a wide and lasting moat — that protect them from the assault of competitors."

## 四、核心投资原则（来自巴菲特原文）

### 股票选择标准（反复出现）
1. **业务可理解**（we can understand）
2. **长期前景有利**（favorable long-term prospects）
3. **管理层诚实且有能力**（competent and honest management）
4. **价格有吸引力**（attractive price，参照私人所有者标准）

### 企业收购标准（反复出现）
1. 大型收购（有合理回报预期）
2. 稳定行业（不是热点但可预测）
3. 管理层诚信有能力
4. 价格合理

## 六、理论溯源

### 继承自格雷厄姆的元素
- 安全边际（Margin of Safety）
- 市场先生的寓言
- 内在价值的概念
- "雪茄烟蒂"投资法（以极低价格买入普通企业）

### 由费舍尔补充的元素
- 集中持仓优质成长股
- 与管理层的"闲聊"（scuttlebutt method）
- 长期持有优秀企业

### 芒格带来的进化
- 从"以好价格买普通企业" → "以合理价格买优质企业"
- 引入"概率权重"思维
- 强调心理因素（人类误判心理学）

## 七、关键原文摘录（来源标注）

### 原文1：关于内在价值（来源：本地素材——巴菲特致股东信，1984）
> "Intrinsic value is the discounted value of the cash that can be taken out of a business during its remaining life. Anyone calculating intrinsic value necessarily comes up with a highly subjective figure that will change both as estimates of future cash flows are revised and as interest rates move. Despite its fuzziness, however, intrinsic value is all-important and is the only logical way to evaluate the relative attractiveness of investments and businesses."

### 原文2：关于市场先生（来源：本地素材——巴菲特致股东信，1987前后）
> "Ben Graham, my friend and teacher, long ago described the mental attitude toward market fluctuations that I believe to be most conducive to investment success. He said that you should imagine market quotations as coming from a remarkably accommodating fellow named Mr. Market who is your partner in a private business. Without fail, Mr. Market appears daily and names a price at which he will either buy your interest or sell you his... The more manic-depressive his behavior, the better for you."

### 原文3：关于买好生意的重要性（来源：本地素材——巴菲特致股东信，1991前后）
> "It must be noted that your Chairman, always a quick study, required only 20 years to recognize how important it was to buy good businesses. In the interim, I searched for 'bargains' — and had the misfortune to find some. My punishment was an education in the economics of short-line farm implement manufacturers."

### 原文4：关于留存收益创造价值（来源：本地素材——巴菲特致股东信，1986前后）
> "We test the wisdom of retaining earnings by assessing whether retention, over time, delivers shareholders at least $1 of market value for each $1 retained. To date, this test has been met."

### 原文5：关于护城河（来源：本地素材——巴菲特致股东信，1993）
> "The key is to find companies that have a wide and lasting moat — that protect them from the assault of competitors."

## 八、调研结论

巴菲特的投资哲学并非一成不变，而是经历了明显的进化：

1. **早期（1950s-1960s）**：完全继承格雷厄姆，以"雪茄烟蒂"和净现金清算值为核心
2. **中期（1970s-1980s）**：开始转向"以合理价格买优质企业"，但仍保留对低估值的偏好
3. **成熟期（1990s至今）**：明确提出"护城河"理论，强调长期持有伟大企业，芒格的影响显著

**不变的核心**：
- 内在价值是唯一衡量标准
- 市场先生是工具而非主人
- 投资即生意（business ownership mindset）
- 理性和耐心是投资成功的必备品质
