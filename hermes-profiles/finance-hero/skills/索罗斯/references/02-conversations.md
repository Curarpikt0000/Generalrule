## 一、Charlie Rose采访系列（1995-2008）

### 1995采访
- **来源**：YouTube - Charlie Rose 1995，频道：Investor Archive
- **可信度**：一手（完整采访转录）
- **关键内容**：
  - "charity is a very corrupting activity"——慈善腐蚀性
  - 1992年英镑交易：投入10亿美元，"most we could have lost would be a few hundred million"
  - 表达对欧洲一体化逆转的担忧（1995年已预见欧洲解体风险）
  - 提到中国："communist regime will be swept out of power by an old-fashioned capitalist bust"
  - 形容自己投资风格："I'm immune because I've now got a young team... I'm just standing behind them, I am coach"

### 2001采访
- **来源**：YouTube - Charlie Rose 2001，时长51:45
- **可信度**：一手（完整采访转录）
- **即兴时刻**：
  - 被问"why should we believe you"——回答："no, I've always been fallible and actually you always felt that way"
  - 承认2000年预言灾难犯了错："i was carried away by forces that i could see building up"
  - 关于普京："not much... he is as yet not that a terribly well-formed personality"
  - 关于以色列："distressed... unless Sharon manages to play a Nixon in China... i don't think it's really in the cards"
  - 想见的历史人物："keynes... i would have liked to meet him"

### 2008采访
- **来源**：YouTube - Charlie Rose 2008，时长31:30
- **可信度**：一手（完整采访转录）
- **即兴表达**：
  - 打断主持人说"breakdown in the prevailing world order"——"you went off at the deep end right but for the pregnant point"
  - 批评格林斯潘："he spoke about irrational exuberance very early on but he stopped... and he was right but he didn't follow through"
  - 说"capital markets don't work"然后补充"they work but but they are imperfect"
  - "maybe the fact that i was able to make 40% a year on my capital had something to do with my knowing this... well other people but okay"

## 六、即兴类比模式

1. **"pushing on a string"**：形容日本货币政策——"keynes mentioned this problem of pushing on a string but there are these holes in the banking system so you can push a lot of string into those holes"

2. **"coach as quarterback"**：描述自己从一线退下——"I'm just standing behind them, I am coach as a quarterback isn't it"

3. **"super bubble"**：描述信贷过度扩张——"starting 1980 with reagan... you had this misconception about markets which are called market fundamentalism"
